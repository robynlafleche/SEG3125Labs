Meet Our Team


Amanda O'Neill

Amanada is one of our senior stylist's here at Scissors Hair Salon. Amanda has taken multiple classes with WELLA, Bumble and Bumble, and Tresemmé in order to improve and perfect her colouring skills, balayage and up-do looks. Amanada prides herself on her attention to detail and meticulous work.

Gerard Boucher

Gerard started his hair styling journey in Madrid, Spain, but his passion for learning led him to Canada, where he became a certified Salon Sebastian Expert. Gerard is also a Great Length Extension Specialist and prides himself on his clean and subtle work. Gerards's energy and laughter is contagious, here at Scissors.

Michelle Maclean

Michelle Maclean was our first employee here at Scissors. Michelle's dedication to her clients and her dedication to her work is second to none. Michelle strives to create customized hairstyles that are tailored to each client. She prides herself on building trust with her clients to form long standing relationships.

Renée Rocan

Renée just recently joined our team as a senior stylist. Renée is a jack of all trades here at Scissors. Her ability to manage all types of hair situations makes her an invaluable asset. She has a focus on precision cutting and prides herself on her attention to detail. Renée's work is always polished and beautiful.
